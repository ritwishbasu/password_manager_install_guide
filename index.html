Action Required: Install a Password Manager

Please complete these steps to ensure your work computer meets security compliance standards.

1

Install an Approved Password Manager

Choose one of the following apps and set up your account:

  • 1Password
  • Bitwarden
  • LastPass
  • Dashlane
💡 Crucial Step: Make sure you also install and activate the Browser Extension in your primary browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, etc.). Vanta uses the extension to verify installation.
2

Keep the Vanta App Active

Ensure the Vanta Device Monitor app is running in the background on your computer. It will automatically scan your system and report back that your device is secure.

✨ You don't need to manually send screenshots or evidence—Vanta handles it automatically!
3

You're All Set!

Once Vanta syncs, your device will automatically update to Compliant status in our dashboard.
